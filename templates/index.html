TNPSC Course (Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission) is an exclusive course created for the Recruitments conducted by Tamil Nadu State Govt. TNPSC recruits candidates for state-owned Orgs’ like TNFUSRC, TN TET, etc. There are plenty of Recruitments conducted by TNPSC Every year.

TET

The Teacher Eligibility Test, known as TET, is the minimum qualification required in India for a person to be eligible for appointment as a teacher for Classes I to VIII. The test is mandatory for teaching jobs in Indian government schools.
